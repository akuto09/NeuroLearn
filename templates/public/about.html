About

Cognitive growth needs
real-time understanding.

Most learning platforms wait for a bad quiz score before doing anything. NeuroLearn was built on a simple idea: mental readiness is a signal too, and it's available before performance ever drops.

The problem we saw

Students study while fatigued far more often than academic systems ever detect. Cognitive overload builds quietly. By the time grades reflect it, weeks of inefficient study have already happened.

What we built instead

NeuroLearn combines EEG-based mental state detection with behavioral learning data — quiz performance, response time, revision patterns — so the platform can act during a session, not after it.

Applications

Where this matters

Higher education

Long, dense material where fatigue-aware pacing protects retention.

Competitive exam prep

High-stakes, high-volume study where overload silently erodes performance.

Professional upskilling

Busy schedules where session efficiency matters more than raw hours.
