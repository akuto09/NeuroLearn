FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need real EEG hardware?

NeuroLearn is designed around consumer-grade, non-invasive EEG headsets. A simulated demo mode is also available so you can explore the platform without hardware.

Is my cognitive data private?

Yes. EEG and behavioral data are tied to your account only, used to build your personal learning profile, and never sold to third parties.

Can teachers see individual EEG data?

Teachers see aggregated, cohort-level readiness trends by default. Individual raw signal access requires explicit learner consent.

What happens if fatigue gets too high?

The adaptive engine shortens sessions, lightens difficulty, and suggests a break — proactively, before comprehension drops.
