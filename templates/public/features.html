Platform

Every part of the system, built around one loop.

Sense cognitive state → interpret it → adapt the lesson → learn from the outcome. Repeat every session.

Sense

EEG + behavioral capture

Non-invasive EEG streams attention, meditation, cognitive load, and fatigue. In parallel, response time, revision frequency, and session length are logged automatically as you study.

Interpret

AI decision engine

A rule-and-model based engine fuses physiological and behavioral signals into a single cognitive-readiness score, updated continuously through a session.

Adapt

Dynamic personalization

Pace, content difficulty, instructional strategy, revision scheduling, and study duration shift automatically — no manual settings required.

Learn

Long-term cognitive profile

Every session refines a persistent profile of how you learn best, so week three is smarter about you than day one.

Also included

A full learning platform underneath

Adaptive tests & assignments

Difficulty recalibrates in real time based on your live cognitive-readiness score.

Progress & retention analytics

Visual breakdowns of mastery, fatigue trends, and revision effectiveness over time.

AI study assistant

A conversational assistant that recommends what to review next based on your current state.

Teacher & admin tooling

Role-based dashboards for course management, cohort analytics, and platform administration.

Smart notifications

Gentle nudges to take a break when fatigue rises, or dive deeper when focus peaks.

Secure by design

Encrypted authentication, role-based permissions, and full audit logging throughout.
